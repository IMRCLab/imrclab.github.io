Motion Planning

This course is jointly developed and held by Dr. Andreas Orthey (Realtime Robotics) and Dr. Wolfgang HÃ¶nig (TU Berlin). It provides a unified perspective on motion planning and includes topics from different research and industry communities. The goal is not only to learn the foundations and theory of currently used approaches, but also to be able to pick and compare the different methods for specific motion planning needs. An important emphasis is the consideration of both geometric and kinodynamic motion planning for the major algorithm types.
